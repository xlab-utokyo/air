Air on Air

ABOUT WATCH PLAY

Dummy Text. Air on Air is an online participatory installation that delivers breath via the Internet and sends bubbles into the distant sky. Visitors can access a website and blow into a microphone to send soap bubbles into a distant land. As they fly into the sky, they are streamed to a browser and viewed from a first-person perspective through a screen. In this age of limited mobility and distance, this work gives us an opportunity to connect with the world beyond the screen with physicality and to recognize anew the invisible existence of air and wind.

About this Website

このウェブサイト上では、Air on Airの観賞と体験ができます。
観賞では、あなたは、他者がリアルタイムに操作している状態を見ることが出来ます。
体験では、あなたは、30秒の間実際に空気を送る事で自分でシャボン玉を送る事が出来ます。
