Concept

Dummy text Air on Air is an online participatory installation that uses the Internet to deliver breath and blow bubbles into the sky at a distance. You can blow bubbles to a remote location by going to the website and blowing into the microphone. In these times of constraints of movement and distance, this experience provides an opportunity to connect with the world beyond the screen, with physicality, and to remind us of the invisible presence of air.

How to Play

You can actually blow bubbles into the sky in the distance at this site. To participate you will need to use a microphone. If you want to check if the microphone is working properly, please refer to "Instruction Mode". To play, select a location from "Choose a location". In this work, data is acquired and used from the microphone. For more information, please see our Privacy Policy.
